Is HVAC Maintenance Part Of Your Routine Cleaning?

by sheraz.ali

Most people heavily invest in a Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (HVAC) system, however, not many people understand the importance of its regular maintenance.

What most people fail to understand is that an HVAC system is no different than a vehicle. Just as you would give attention to the maintenance of your car so it can run and function smoothly and properly, you need to treat your HVAC system with the same level of care.

For your further assistance, here are some major reasons why you should not neglect the maintenance of your HVAC.

Lower Utility Bills Than Usual

If your HVAC system is properly maintained and your house is fully insulated, you can save a significant amount of money on electricity, cooling, and heating costs. Your best bet to take this up by employing the services of professionals. They are well-versed with all the technicalities and can guide you properly.

Healthy and Clean Air

The purpose of an HVAC system is not just to keep your house cool or warm. The unit also circulates the air in your house. If you have been neglecting the chore of having a professional come over to clean the coils and filters, you can only imagine the kind of air that is ventilating in your house.

Make sure you regularly get your unit checked as it can become a den for mould, dirt, and bacteria, which can be transmitted into your home and create breathing problems for your family.

Increases the Lifespan of the Unit

It goes without saying that the more you emphasize on the regular maintenance of your HVAC system, the longer it will serve you. On average, a well-kept HVAC system can last for more than 15 years. Considering how much money you have spent on the installation, you may as well want to make sure the system operates efficiently throughout its lifespan.

Fewer Chances of Requiring Emergency Repairs

Every so often, an HVAC system may require emergency repairs. When the winter season arrives, you don’t want to be caught in a situation where you are freezing due to malfunctioning of the heating unit. Only if you had ensured regular inspections from the professionals of a cleaning service in Dubai, you wouldn’t be facing the issue or spending hefty amounts on its urgent repairs.

So, if you have been inconsiderate about your HVAC system, now is the time to take action. You will realize that your money is being well-spent when your unit works longer than you had expected.

Cleaning Hacks for Pet Owners from Expert Cleaners in Dubai

by sheraz.ali

From eliminating odors to cleaning fur, every pet owner understands the challenges involved in keeping the house clean. No doubt, your furry cat or dog brings happiness to you but sometimes, regardless of how long you try to clean, you may still spot some pet hair or dander in the house.

So, while the furry realm is indeed beneficial for you and your family’s mental health and mood, let’s take a look at some nifty pet and home cleaning hacks shared by cleaning experts.

Essential oils to mask the odor
Lavender oil or any other essential oil helps in calming dogs. All you need is to add a few drops of oil to a cup of water. Fill the spray bottle with the solution and spray it on surfaces where you would like to get rid of pet odors. However, too much use of essential oil may give you the opposite results so use it as much as required. Remember, a few essential oils like peppermint, tea tree or more may be toxic to pets.

Duct tape to clean pet hair
Duct tape works wonders when it’s about removing pet hair from carpets, furniture, and vehicle seats. Duct tapes are sticky which makes them faster than vacuum. Simply wrap a cloth using duct tape and rub on corners to get rid of pet sheds. You may also wrap the duct tape on a paint roller and make it a pet hair remover. Keep the sticky side outside and roll it over the carpet, furniture, or vehicle seats to remove pet sheds.

Enzymatic cleaner to get rid of pet stains
Take enzymatic cleaner and clean pet stains or odor. Particularly, for pet owners, enzymatic cleaners work best. These solutions help in neutralizing and removing bacteria found in pet urine. Also, they generate healthy bacteria that kill harmful bacteria and remove all types of stains and odor.

Gift your pet a bandana
Indeed, pet bandanas are popular these days. It is a new fashion statement for dogs especially. Many studies suggest that bandanas are good for human and dog health. It catches loose hair and cuts down pet shedding up to a great extent. It also protects the pet from sunburns when outside.

Besides these hacks what really matters is to hire one of the most professional and reliable cleaning services in Dubai that is well-versed about pet cleaning and performs the job without hurting your pet.

Residential Cleaning Services – Keep Your Home Safe and The Virus Away!

by sheraz.ali

The Covid-19 has taken over the entire world. From washing hands every now and then to use disinfectants regularly is now a new normal. Considering the deadly impact of this novel virus, world experts recommend that you must now take additional steps to keep yourself and your house free of germs.

The Recommended Disinfecting Routine to Fight off Coronavirus

In order to ensure cleaner house and virus-free utensils, you must practice a daily routine of disinfecting.

For your assistance, here are some suggestions to keep your kitchen as well as bathroom free of germs.

In The Kitchen

Disinfect and thoroughly clean sink hardware, countertops, appliance handles, cabinet pulls and cutting boards

Use dishcloths that can be washed easily and on the hot cycle. Also, replace the dishcloths and towels each day

Clean all spills immediately to avoid attracting bacteria and more dirt

Keep the wastebaskets empty and dispose all the garbage daily. You may also spray the trash can using disinfectant every day.

In The Bathroom

  • Disinfect and clean the bathroom handles and faucets
  • Keep wastebaskets empty and also disinfect them regularly
  • Spray on the flush handle and toilet with sanitizing wipes or disinfectants
  • Avoid using wash clothes and bath towels more than just once to fully prevent the bacteria and germ growth

Weekly Disinfecting Routine to Stay Safe from Coronavirus

In The Kitchen

  • Wash all kitchen sink strainers using the dishwasher
  • Remove all the range knobs and oven and hand wash them
  • Disinfect and clean the kitchen sinks

In The Bathroom

  • Wash the toothbrushes and toothbrush holder and disinfect them. In case they can be washed using a dishwasher, simply run them all through, along with your dishes.
  • Disinfect and wash your shower and bathtub, including the handles and shower doors. In case you also have a machine washable shower curtain, throw it in your washer.

Other Rooms

  • Use hot water to wash the bedding. Avoid shaking out the sheets and blankets before washing in order to avoid the spread of germs and dust.
  • Mop all the hard surface floors and regularly vacuum carpets.
  • Disinfect mouse, computer keyboards, light switches, remote controls and cell phones. Also, don’t forget to turn off the electricity or other electronics before disinfecting and cleaning.

Certainly it is a lot of work. If you think that you don’t have time to practice the recommended routine, don’t fret and employ one of the best residential cleaning company dubai. After all, you don’t need to fight off the virus alone!
